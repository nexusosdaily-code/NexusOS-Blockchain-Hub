Max Planck discovered that energy is quantized:


- **E** = Energy (Joules)
- **h** = Planck's constant (6.62607015 × 10⁻³⁴ J·s)
- **f** = Frequency (Hz)

**Nobel Prize 1918**: "For the discovery of energy quanta"

**Key Insight**: Energy is not continuous — it exists in discrete packets determined by frequency. Higher oscillation frequency = higher energy.

### 2.2 Einstein (1905): Mass-Energy Equivalence

Albert Einstein revealed that mass and energy are interchangeable:

```
E = mc²
```

Where:
- **E** = Energy (Joules)
- **m** = Mass (kg)
- **c** = Speed of light (299,792,458 m/s)

**Nobel Prize 1921**: "For the discovery of the law of the photoelectric effect"

**Key Insight**: Mass is a form of energy. Energy can become mass; mass can become energy.

**Key Discoveries**:

- Energy density oscillates at **twice the wave frequency** (u ∝ sin²(ωt))
- All electromagnetic phenomena are manifestations of oscillation

**Key Insight**: The universe is built on oscillating fields. Maxwell's equations describe how oscillation propagates through space.
